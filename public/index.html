Weekly Proof

How it Works:

1 The Baseline

Each Runner logged a baseline. The app used pace and heart rate to estimate their full effort 5k (175 avg. BPM). This is each runner's official starting line.

2 The Goal

The 5K world record is 12:35. Close your gap to the world record by the most, relative to your baseline. (Accounts for the increased difficulty of shaving time at faster paces).

3 The Race

The runners still race straight up, but only for pride. The $500 goes to the runner who closes a greater percentage of their world record gap by the Thanksgiving Turkey Trot.

Equal relative effort results in a perfect tie. No sandbagging. May the more disciplined athlete win.
